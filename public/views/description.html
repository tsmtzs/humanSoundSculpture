Human Sound Sculpture

Description

Human Sound Sculpture is a piece of performance art for public space. A group of people arranged in a line is moving towards a point. The spectator faces a snapshot of this proccess. Human Sound Sculpture is based on the text score

Night.
People with wooden faces.
Moving towards the same point
One behind the other.
Close enough or far apart.
Time at still.

Part of the piece is the ambient soundscape. Along with it a random endless melody unfolds. This melody is distributed on the devices of performers.

Human Sound Sculpture despite it's static form, deals with motion. The relentless motion of Time. Which interwines with the footsteps of passers-by. With the roar of the city. With the thoughts, dreams, memories and aspirations of people the standing frozen in the row.

Tassos Tsesmetzis
